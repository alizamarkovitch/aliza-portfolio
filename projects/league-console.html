League Console

Empower business users with autonomous app configuration

Overview

This project was completed while I was working at League, a digital healthcare company that empowers health providers to deliver exceptional digital health experiences. Our latest project, the League Console, not only streamlines the in-app configuration process but also marks a significant step towards digital autonomy, reducing dependency on technical staff for daily operations.

The Challenge

League's 2024 goal is to offer a platform-centric service for clients to scale effectively and work autonomously. The current fragmented, error-prone configuration process demands technical expertise, slowing down campaign deployment and impacting client satisfaction. Our objective is to enable self-service and reduce reliance on internal teams.

Team and Role

As the Lead Designer of the Content & Configuration Tools pod, I spearheaded the design of the configuration interface, which reduced setup time by 40% and significantly improved user satisfaction. I worked alongside a Product Manager and a development team, reporting to the Director of Product Design.

Process

Discovery

Conducted nine semi-structured interviews with primary users from five internal capability teams to identify pain points in the existing configuration process.

Ideation

Created comprehensive information architecture and conducted contextual inquiry to understand user needs and workflows.

Validation

Developed and tested prototypes with users, gathering feedback to refine the design and improve usability.

Implementation

Collaborated closely with development team to ensure accurate implementation of design specifications.

Solution

The League Console aligns with League's strategic vision by centralizing and simplifying configuration. This intuitive tool enables business users to manage member experiences independently. Its form-based interface eliminates the need for coding, allowing non-technical staff to create and modify campaigns without IT support. The scalable framework reduces implementation effort and minimizes technical barriers for clients.

Results

The League Console significantly enhances the ability of business users to configure the League Member experience autonomously and efficiently, reducing admin time by 40%. By providing a centralized tool that accommodates non-technical users and unifies tooling and documentation, we have improved the configuration workflow, reducing dependency on technical staff and the League team.
